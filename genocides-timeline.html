Modern Genocides: A Temporal Spatial Narrative

An educational exploration of genocide through time and space

About This Project

This interactive timeline maps major genocides of the modern era (20th-21st centuries) for educational and comparative analysis. Click on any event to learn more about the historical context, scale, and ongoing impact.

Note: This is a prototype showing the core timeline functionality. Future versions will include 3D spatial visualization and expanded educational content.
